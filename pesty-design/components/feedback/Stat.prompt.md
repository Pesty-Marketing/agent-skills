Oversized result metric — the case-study numbers Pesty leads with. Figure in Poppins extrabold, defaults to Pesty Red.

```jsx
<Stat value="$100,000" label="of monthly ad spend saved after 6 months of SEO" icon="trending-up" />
<Stat value="329" label="new recurring customers at $72.04 CAC" align="center" />
<Stat value="$24.24" label="cost per qualified lead from SEO" onDark />
```

Set `accent={false}` for an ink-colored figure, `onDark` on dark sections, `align="center"` to center.
